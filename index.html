✦ Features

Everything you need to eat smarter

From food search to body tracking — NutriLyze has every tool you need to reach your health goals.

🔍

Smart Food Search

Search from USDA verified foods. Macros auto-filled — calories, protein, carbs, fat in one click.

🎯

Personalized Goals

Set weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain goals. Daily calorie & protein targets calculated from your BMR + TDEE.

📊

Macro Dashboard

Visual donut chart showing carbs, protein and fat breakdown every day. Progress bars keep you on track.

💧

Water Tracking

Log water intake with quick-add buttons (150ml, 250ml, 500ml, 750ml). Daily 2500ml goal with progress bar.

📏

Body Measurements

Track weight, height, BMI and body fat over time. BMI auto-calculated with ideal weight range display.

🔒

Secure & Private

Your account is protected with industry-standard encryption. Role-based access ensures your personal data stays yours — always private.
